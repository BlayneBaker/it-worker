Divvies

Divvies is a company that is near and dear to my heart. Being a customer and supporter of the brand myself, it made perfect sense to become a marketer of the brand as well. Therefore, I have created 4 mock advertisments for Divvies. Each ad showcases a different product the company offers. My aim with these mock ads was to bring awareness to new consumers that amazing treats exist that are made without many of the common food allergins found in normal, everyday products. Divvies makes simple, yet delicious, food for vegans and those with dietary restrictions.

Potsdam Humane Society

Animal welfare has always been something I found myself extremely passionate about ever since I was a little girl. To me, pets are creatures who bless the lives of so many and consistantly brighten the world around them. I believe this because my 3 cats have greatly enhanced my well-being in life and never cease to put a smile on my face. I knew it was time for me to become the voice for these loving creatures and help them find the love they deserve in this world.

I chose to create 3 mock ads for The Potsdam Humane Society because of my love for these animals. Pets will love someone unconditionally and each one deserves a forever home where they can receive that love back in tenfold. My ads focus on prompting adoption of these animals by demonstrating their need for you and your need for them. No family is complete without a family pet!

Dove

Growing up, Dove was the brand my mom consistantly refered to me when it came to beauty products. Women of all ages have come to love this brand, and it doesn't surprise me why. Dove is a company that truly cares about its consumer, and that fact has been made abundantly clear with the launch of its newest ad campaign entitled, "My Beauty, My Say."

When I saw this campaign, I instantly knew I could make a benenfical contribution to the narrative. I decided in that moment to make 3 mock ads of my own advertising this campaign to empower women of all ages to view themselves as unstoppable and powerful. I loved this message, and I like to think my ads reflect that personal passion.

Marketing Blog

Mickey Mouse has been a beloved icon since 1928. He has adorned numerous products worldwide and has spearheaded Disney's marketing empire. Most consumers know this little mouse as a cute and innocent childhood character, but what if I told you Mickey Mouse has a dark side?

My blog entitled, "The Impossible Hijack of Mickey Mouse" examines Mickey Mouse's dark side as it pertains to copyright law, and offers potential solutions to fix this dilemna come 2020.

Alumni Relations

Since March 2016, I have worked per diem for Clarkson University's Alumni Relations. I specialize in the writing and editing of brochures, press releases, and annual giving letters. Each project aims to foster alumni-student engagment and increase university donations.
